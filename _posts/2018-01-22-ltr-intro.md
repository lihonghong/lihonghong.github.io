### Learning to Rank主要有3种算法：
1. Pointwise: Subset Ranking, McRank, Prank, OC SVM
2. Pairwise: Ranking SVM, RankBoost, RankNet, GBRank, IR SVM, Lambda Rank, LambdaMart
3. Listwise: ListNet, ListMLE, AdaRank, SVM MAP, Soft Rank
